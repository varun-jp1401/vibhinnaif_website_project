About Vibhinna

Vibhinna India Foundation is a Section-8 organization dedicated to creating harmony between people and the environment through sustainable community-driven initiatives.

Founded with a vision to restore ecological balance and empower communities, we work across multiple sectors including environmental conservation, education, rural development, and health. Our approach combines traditional wisdom with modern sustainable practices to create lasting positive impact.

Through collaboration with local communities, government bodies, educational institutions, and corporate partners, we implement programs that address pressing environmental and social challenges while building resilient, self-sustaining communities.

Our Focus Areas

Ecology & Environment

Large-scale tree plantation drives, biodiversity conservation, urban greening initiatives, and restoration of degraded ecosystems across Karnataka.

Education

Environmental education programs in schools, skill development workshops, and awareness campaigns to build an environmentally conscious generation.

Rural Livelihood

Supporting sustainable farming practices, creating market linkages for rural produce, and empowering local communities through skill training and resource access.

Health

Community health initiatives, promoting wellness through connection with nature, and supporting healthcare facilities with green infrastructure.

Our Approach

  • Work with local communities to understand their needs and co-create solutions
  • Design long-term sustainable solutions that don't require constant external support
  • Bridge people and nature through programs that benefit both
  • Create measurable impact with transparent monitoring and reporting
  • Foster partnerships across sectors for collective action
  • Use native species and traditional ecological knowledge in restoration projects
  • Build capacity within communities for self-governance and resource management
